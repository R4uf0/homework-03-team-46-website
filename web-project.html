How websites work?

Welcome to our Website Project! This platform illustrates how websites are constructed and operated. Websites utilize three essential tools: HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for interactivity. When you visit a website, your browser fetches all these elements from a server and displays them for you. In this project, we highlight the process of creating our site, from basic pages to exciting interactive features and code art. You can also explore our Codecademy accounts on this page to see our progress in learning. Join us and learn how websites are brought to life!
